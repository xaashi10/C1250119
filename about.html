About Horizon Realty

Your trusted real estate partner in Mogadishu

Our Mission

Horizon Realty was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to help families and businesses find the perfect property in Mogadishu and surrounding areas. We combine local expertise with professional service to make every transaction smooth and transparent.

Whether you're looking for a cozy apartment, a spacious villa, or a commercial space, our team is dedicated to understanding your needs and finding the right match. We believe in building lasting relationships with our clients, one property at a time.
